Crack the Safe

A game by Tyler George

Gameplay

  • Wear headphones for the best experience.
  • Timer: Counts down. Don't let it hit 0!
  • Dial and Numbers: Turn the dial and flip the numbers until you hear a distinct sound. That's how you know it's the right position.
  • Open button: Press the open button when you think you've got the code.
  • Red lights: A red light will light up when trying to open with the wrong input. Three strikes and you're out!
