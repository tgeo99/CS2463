Crack the Safe

A game by Tyler George

Hardware

The Arduino was set up to mirror the screen.

As you turn the dial, the dial on screen will turn. Similarly, the numbers on screen will increase as the three middle buttons are pressed. The right button enters the combination.

The three red middle lights will light up as you enter the wrong combination. Don't light them all!
