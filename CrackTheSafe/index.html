Crack the Safe

A game by Tyler George

Overview

Crack the Safe is a p5.js, Tone.js, and Arduino-based puzzle game. The objective is to enter the correct combination of both dial and numbers to crack the safe. You get three attempts, and the clock is ticking before the cops catch you! Listen quickly and carefully to find the combination.
